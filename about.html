About Me

Dr Shannon Martin

Clinical Psychologist and director of Power Psychology.

Experienced, evidence-based and human.

Shannon has worked in corrections, district health, physical rehabilitation and neuropsychology settings.

After a decade working across public and government organisations, Shannon established Power Psychology. She has extensive experience delivering evidence-based interventions and draws predominantly on EMDR and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy.

Her areas of interest include trauma and PTSD, anxiety, maternal mental health and the interaction between emotional wellbeing, cognition and everyday functioning.
