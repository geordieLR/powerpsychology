Referral pathways

Start with the route that matches the referral.

Funding, contract eligibility and current availability must be confirmed before an appointment is offered.

Important: this prototype does not state that every ACC referral can be accepted. Contract scope, eligibility, referral authority and availability require confirmation by Power Psychology.
1

Prospective client

Let us know whether you are enquiring privately or have been advised that an ACC-funded pathway may apply. Do not include a detailed trauma history in the website form.

2

Health professional

Provide a concise referral purpose, funding pathway if known, and the best contact details for follow-up.

3

Case manager or funder

Confirm the applicable contract or approval pathway, requested service, reporting expectations and contact person.

Information to confirm before referral

  • The service being requested and clinical fit.
  • The funding or contract pathway.
  • Who is authorised to refer or approve the service.
  • Current capacity and expected timing.
  • Consent, information-sharing and reporting requirements.
  • Whether in-person or telehealth delivery is suitable.

Privacy at the first contact

Only share the minimum information needed to establish the referral type and arrange a safe follow-up. Detailed clinical information should be exchanged through an agreed secure process.
