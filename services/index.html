Individual therapy

Sessions are offered to adults aged 18 and over, in person or online. The first phase explores current difficulties, relevant history, goals and the most suitable treatment plan.

50 minutes | Current published fee: $239

Therapy is collaborative and requires effort both within and outside sessions. Frequency and duration depend on clinical needs, goals and progress.

Fees, availability and funding arrangements should be confirmed directly before booking.

Neuropsychological assessment

60 minutes | Current published fee: $239

An assessment can include clinical interview, review of personal and health history, and cognitive or psychological testing. The purpose is to understand cognitive strengths and difficulties and inform diagnosis or rehabilitation.

Traumatic brain injury is an area of speciality.

Professional supervision

50 minutes | Current published fee: $230

Supervision is available for early- to mid-career psychologists and other health professionals, including GPs, counsellors, nurses, social workers and occupational therapists.

Power Psychology has experience working across ACC contracts.

Cancellation policy

Power Psychology requires 48 hours’ notice for cancellation. The current published policy states that cancellations within 24–48 hours may incur an $80 fee, and cancellations within 24 hours may incur the full session fee.

Please confirm the current policy when arranging an appointment.
